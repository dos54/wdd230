Turning Alaska Site Plan

Site Name

Ketchikan Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the city in which the chamber of commerce operates.

Purpose

The Ketchikan Chamber of Commerce serves the local community by providing services to its local businesses, such as facilitating the creation of connections and partnerships between businesses.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find information about upcoming events?
  • How can I contact board members?
  • How has the population grown recently?
  • Why should I invest in Ketchikan?
